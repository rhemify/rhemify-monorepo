Rhemos is the verifiable payment layer for agentic commerce. Positioning evolved from "treasury intelligence" → "fleet control engine" → "the home of agentic commerce."

**Core positioning: Route. Govern. Verify.**
- **Route** — any standard (x402, MPP, L402, AP2, ACP), any chain, cheapest path. The "Jupiter for agent payments" entry point.
- **Govern** — fleet policy engine, intelligence rules, per-agent controls. The enterprise trust layer.
- **Verify** — every payment decision hashed on Solana. Provable, immutable, replayable. The permanent record. This is what makes Rhemos "the home" — your history lives here.

**Key differentiators:**
- Multi-standard payment routing (8+ standards)
- Decision trace layer — captures WHY, not just WHAT. Replayable.
- Intelligence layer — rules engine, vendor scores, anomaly detection, auto-actions
- Verifiable on Solana — trace hashes in PDAs, provable vendor reputation
- MCP-native — drop-in for OpenClaw, Claude Code, Codex, Cursor
- Policy templates — shareable proven fleet configurations (inspired by AgentCircle's Strategy Packs concept)

**Competitors:** Sponge (custodial, single standard), MCPay (x402 only, C4 accelerator), Latinum (wallet model), Mercantill (multi-sig governance), AgentCash (curated marketplace)

**Team (4 people):**

- Wei Hup (Aaron) — external integrations (AgentCard, Jupiter, CCTP, onboarding CLI)

**Target:** Colosseum Frontier (Apr 6 - May 11, 2026). Tracks: Infrastructure + Stablecoins.

**Tech stack:** TanStack Start frontend, Go backend, TypeScript SDK + MCP server, OWS + Privy signing, PostgreSQL, Solana (settlement), Helius RPC, Jupiter swap, CCTP bridge

**Positioning evolution (this conversation):**
- Started as "treasury intelligence layer"
- Shifted to "fleet control engine for agentic commerce" (dropped "treasury" — too finance-y)
- Explored "Jupiter for agents" (routing layer analogy)
- Final: "The home of agentic commerce" with "Route. Govern. Verify." framework
- The verifiable layer (trace hashes on Solana) is the stickiest differentiator — it creates a permanent record that competitors can't replicate and operators can't leave
